I am a research student in Secure Systems at the University of Surrey. My PhD is under the supervision of Helen Treharne and Steve Schneider, and is supported by the EPSRC and Thales UK. As part of the PhD I completed a placement at Thales UK Research and Technology in Reading, and Thales eSecurity in Cambridge.

My current research interests are mainly in the formal verification of security protocols, with a focus on how to model "Trusted Computing" symbolically.
